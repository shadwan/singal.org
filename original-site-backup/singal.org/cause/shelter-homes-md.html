Where We Work

Interfaith Works Shelter is located at Rockville, Maryland.

The Approach

Emergency Shelter and Rehabilitation:

The Interfaith Works Women's Center is a 70-bed short-term emergency shelter serving approximately 300 women per year.

Case managers work with clients towards a rapid exit housing plan from the shelter by referring them to housing programs, vocational programs, making medical referrals, mental health referrals, and connection to social services benefits.
