**Partner:** Children's Business Fair  
**Location:** Rockville, Maryland (local community)

**Role:** Premier Sponsor + Training Partner

**Description:**  
Singal Foundation has a passion to inspire the next generation of entrepreneurs. Regardless of background, ideology or industry – most people in the world agree that entrepreneurial innovation positively impacts society and the world.
