# Our Initiative in Disaster Relief

**Hero Tagline:** We are committed to providing tangible relief to those affected by natural disasters.

## Project Listing

### 1. Hurricane Harvey Business Fund - Texas, USA

**Location:** Houston, Texas, USA  
**Disaster:** Hurricane Harvey (2017)  
**Grant Total:** $48,000  
**Grants Given:** 7 small business owners

**Description:**  
The Singal Foundation gave grants to seven small business owners whose businesses were devastated by the hurricane. The businesses that were given grants included a boutique, real estate firm, chiropractic clinic, fashion accessory brand, a wedding venue, pre-school and a gym.

**Image:** `ds-img.jpg` (Houston hurricane impact/relief photo)

### 2. COVID-19 Fund 2020 - Global Outreach

**Location:** Global (22 countries)  
**Disaster:** COVID-19 Pandemic (2020)  
**Partner:** Lurn, Inc  
**Grant Range:** $50-$500 per grant  
**Total Raised:** $60,182

**Description:**  
Singal Foundation in partnership with Lurn, Inc started the COVID-19 Entrepreneurship Fund in March 2020. The purpose of the fund was to provide grants ranging from $50 to $500 to small-business owners who were negatively impacted by COVID-19.

**Image:** `covid.jpg` (COVID-19 relief context photo)
