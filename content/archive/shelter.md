# Our Initiative in Shelter Homes

**Hero Tagline:** We believe in giving everyone access to a stable and safe environment in which they can grow and thrive.

## Project Listing

### 1. Compassion Asia, Nepal

**Partner:** Compassion Asia  
**Location:** Jumla District, Nepal  
**Grant:** $10,000 (2016)

**Description:**  
In 2016, the Singal Foundation donated $10,000 to Compassion Asia, a shelter home in Nepal for visually impaired and blind children. This home cares for fifteen to seventeen kids at a time. The children's home is located in the Jumla district of Nepal.

**Image:** `sh-img1.jpg?v=3`

**CTA:** More Information → `/cause/shelter-homes.html`

---

### 2. Interfaith Works Women's Center, USA

**Partner:** Interfaith Works  
**Location:** Montgomery County, Maryland, USA

**Description:**  
The Singal Foundation is headquartered in Gaithersburg, Maryland. As part of our community outreach program, the Singal Foundation has partnered with Interfaith Works, a shelter home for women in Montgomery county, Maryland. The Singal Foundation prepares, serves and delivers meals to the shelter.

**Image:** `img/sh-img-md.jpg`

**CTA:** More Information → `/cause/shelter-homes-md.html`

---

### 3. SEAL Ashram, India

**Partner:** SEAL Ashram  
**Location:** Mumbai, India  
**Grant:** $2,000/month (ongoing)

**Description:**  
The Singal Foundation has committed to caring for 36 children at the SEAL Ashram shelter home in Mumbai, India. A grant of $2000 a month from the Singal Foundation helps fund the kids' food, clothing, shelter, education and medical needs.

**Image:** `sa.jpg`

**CTA:** More Information → `/cause/shelters.html`
