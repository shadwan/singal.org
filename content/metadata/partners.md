# Partner Organizations

## Primary Partners

### 1. Dream Centers / ARC Schools
**Initiative:** Education  
**Location:** Mumbai, India  
**Founded:** Not specified (schools started 2011+)  
**Focus:** Self-sustainable schools in slum communities

**Founders:**
- Ian Hendricks (trains women entrepreneurs, mentioned in images)
- Naomi Hendricks (helps students, mentioned in images)

**Program Details:**

- Support classes for mainstreamed students

### 2. Compassion Asia
**Initiative:** Shelter Homes  
**Location:** Jumla District, Nepal  
**Founded:** Operations since April 2009  
**Focus:** Blind and visually impaired children

**Program Details:**
- Residential shelter home
- Capacity: 15-17 children
- Special education for blind students
- Braille books (purchased from Kathmandu)
- Trained teacher (salary paid by Compassion Asia)
- Partnership with Nepali government

**Singal Foundation Contribution:**
- $10,000 grant (2016)
- Supports basic necessities and education

**Outcomes:** Dramatic improvements in living conditions, health, and emotional well-being since 2009

### 3. Village Enterprise
**Initiative:** Entrepreneurship  
**Location:** East Africa (Kenya, Uganda, Tanzania)  
**Founded:** 1987  
**Focus:** Micro-enterprise development, poverty reduction

**Program Details:**
- 1-year Graduation program
- Groups of 3 entrepreneurs per business
- $150 seed capital per business (grants, not loans)
- 9 months of training
- Business Savings Groups (BSGs) of ~30 entrepreneurs
- Local business mentors
- Focus on women and youth
- Target: People living on less than $1.90/day

**Track Record:**
- 44,000+ businesses started
- 175,000+ East Africans trained
- Research-backed proven impact (multiple studies)

**Singal Foundation Contribution:**
- $25,000 grant (2017)
- 150 entrepreneurs trained
- 50 businesses started
- ~1,000 people impacted (children, women, men)
- Funded Sabwani Marinda Village

**Business Types:** Livestock, farming, retail stores, restaurants, tailoring, beekeeping

### 4. SEAL Ashram
**Initiative:** Medical Relief & Shelter Homes  
**Location:** Mumbai, India  
**Focus:** Destitute individuals from streets and railway stations

**Program Details:**
- Rescue operations (streets & railway stations)
- Comprehensive care: food, clothing, shelter, healthcare, education
- Hospital construction project (in progress as of Dec 2020)
- Serves people with HIV/AIDS, tuberculosis, chronic illnesses
- Current capacity: 265+ residents
- Planned hospital capacity: 200 patients

**Singal Foundation Contribution:**

**Medical Relief:**
- $55,000 total (as of December 2020)
- Hospital construction funding
- Free medical treatment for residents and anyone unable to afford care

**Shelter Homes:**
- $2,000/month ongoing support
- Caring for 36 children
- Food, clothing, shelter, education, medical needs

**Why Needed:** Many hospitals refuse treatment to patients with infectious diseases due to fear/lack of training

- Children create products/services, develop brands, marketing strategies

**Singal Foundation Contribution:**

**Product Examples:** Hand-made jewelry, bath bombs, re-imagined stuffed animals, recipe cards, neck ties, face painting, knitting lessons, specialty lemonade
